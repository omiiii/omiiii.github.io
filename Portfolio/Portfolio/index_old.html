Hi, I'm Nayomi.

I just want to code pretty things.

I love programming. Simple as that. I aspire to specialize in front-end development. Though, right now, I mostly work with Python and Java. In my free time, I deal with HTML and CSS to create nice webpages.
Just as writing beautiful code is important, so is creating beautiful designs. I had a keen eye for color and patterns, so I put them to use. Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator are my weapons. I just need a project to attack.
My eyes are drawn to appealing designs and my ears are drawn to exceptional sounds. Being an ex-orchestra member, I love playing and listening to music. Want to hear? Turn your ears here.

Hello!

I'm a Computational Media major at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I study a combination of computer science and digital media — my favorite subjects. I aspire to specialize in User Interface/User Experience development. In the meantime, I'm experimenting with Android app development, Ruby, and Javascript. I currently live in Atlanta, GA, but I am originally from Detroit, MI. I adore graphic design, travelling, video games, and cartoons. I'm also a pretty nifty singer and musician, who plays guitar, violin, piano, mandolin, and ukulele. If you want to contact me, just shoot me an email.
